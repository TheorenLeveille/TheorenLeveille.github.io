COMP 1113: Applied Mathematics

COMP 1712: Biz Analysis and System Design

Comp 1712 studies the concepts, processes, and tools that professionals use to
plan and develop information systems to industry standards.

COMP 1116: Business Communications 1

Comp 1116 focuses on learning how to be a professional and efficient communicator
in the workplace.

COMP 1100: CST Program Fundamentals

Comp 1100 focuses on topics of importance to new and continuing students on
how to balance school and related activities to enhance their chances of
success in their academic careers.

COMP 1536: Intro to Web Development

Comp1536 focuses on web design and programming, with an emphasis on good

Comp 1510 is a hands-on Python programming course, which introduce the

COMP 1930: Projects 1

Comp 1930 is course where students will learn to work in a collaborative
